BROKEN BEACH:
A BEAUTIFUL NATURAL SCENERY

Ironman / 7 Dec 2023

Introduction:

 Broken Beach, located on the stunning Indonesian island of Nusa Penida, is a natural wonder that captivates visitors with its unique and awe-inspiring beauty. This extraordinary geological formation, like Mount Kinabalu, is a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers. Broken Beach is a testament to the remarkable forces of nature, and it offers an unforgettable experience for those who venture to explore its wonders.

 Broken Beach's claim to fame lies in its mesmerizing natural beauty and geological marvel. This remarkable coastal formation features a natural bridge-like arch that encircles a crystal-clear lagoon, creating a mesmerizing and surreal sight. The archway provides a breathtaking frame for the deep blue waters of the Indian Ocean beyond, making it a perfect spot for photographers and nature enthusiasts.

 The unique formation of Broken Beach is the result of millions of years of erosion, as the relentless forces of the ocean have carved out this natural wonder. It stands as a testament to the incredible power of nature, showcasing the intricate dance between land and sea.

Insights:

 Nusa Penida is a beautiful island near Bali, and I had the chance to visit a place there called Broken Beach. It was amazing!

We got there by boat and then drove through the island's green hills to reach Broken Beach. When we arrived, I was stunned by the view. The beach was inside a big circle of cliffs, and there was a hole in the rocks where the sea came in. The water was a bright blue color, and it looked like a painting. We climbed down some steps to get to the beach. I couldn't swim there because the water was rough, but I enjoyed the view and the sound of the waves hitting the cliffs. Birds flew around the cliffs, and the place felt really special. We stayed for a while, took lots of pictures, and explored the area. Nearby, there was another spot called Angel's Billabong, which had a beautiful natural pool with clear green water. I took a quick swim there before leaving. As the sun went down, it made the cliffs look even more beautiful. Broken Beach reminded me of how amazing nature is, and I left feeling grateful for this hidden paradise.

Recommended Equipment and Tips:

  1. Footwear: Sturdy, non-slip hiking shoes are advisable for exploring the rocky terrain.
  2. Camera: Don't forget your camera or smartphone to capture the breathtaking views.
  3. Respect Nature: Remember to tread lightly and leave no trace to protect this fragile ecosystem.
  4. Timing: Plan your visit during the dry season, typically from April to October, to avoid the rainy weather and enjoy a more comfortable experience.

Budget:

  1. Entrance Fees and Tours: Approximately IDR 14,000 - IDR 28,000.
  2. Guided tours to explore Nusa Penida: IDR 420,000 - IDR 1,120,000
  3. Local transportation (scooter rental or taxis): IDR 140,000 - IDR 420,000 per day
  4. Ferry from Bali to Nusa Penida: IDR 140,000 - IDR 280,000 (round trip).
  5. Accommodation: From IDR 300,000 - IDR 2,250,000 depends on it's level of luxury.
